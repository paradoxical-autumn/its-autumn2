Heya! I'm autumn (aka paradoxical-autumn or paradox or justAnotherParadox... the names continue...) I'm a programmer and musician from the UK who likes messing around with technology.

Outside of that, I enjoy photography, drawing, 3D animating, graphics design and trying my hand at new creative tasks.

Ever since I was a young person, I've been fascinated by the digital world mainly thanks to unrestricted internet access. Oh god.

Music

My love of music started even before that. Apparently, when I was probably 2 I was dancing to the anthems of the time and trying my best to create music with whatever I could find. Maybe when I was about 7, my dad had introduced me to an old app called "launchpad" (I think that was the name) which allowed me to create quick loops of music using premade samples. By the time I was 10, I was messing with the demo for FL Studio and, well... not getting very far as I wasn't allowed to download random files onto the family computer. Y'know, "random files" includes virtual instruments so I was basically stuck with the default set in FL at the time.

FURRY TRASH

Yeah, I might be one. Just maybe. It's not that important though. I don't commonly associate with the fandom, I just choose to represent myself online as my character. I found out about furries in 2019 when some of my (now also furry) friends were hating on furries as a whole and I, in my innocence, had no idea what a furry was. So, I asked them about it and heard a horribly incorrect ramble about what they are and thought "no that can't be right..."

That night, I went on the internet, looked into what furries are and ohnowhathaveidone. The next day, I had a protogen OC. However, I abandoned that character in 2022 when I made my second character, "autumn" (I'll get back to that name later) who was my first "official" fursona (I hate typing that word.) 2023 happened and I made my second - much more recognisable - character, Paradox (hey I wonder why that name sounds familiar).

Oh and to the uneducated people, I'm not one of the "weird" (up to your definition) furries. I don't do/watch/look at/partake in/whatever else anything /bad/, I just simply exist with weird animal characters.

Resonite

As of about the end of 2023, I found a quirky little VR game called Resonite. Most people would compare it to VRChat as they're both social VR games but Resonite has a larger focus on tinkering and creating things. You literally create the worlds, avatars, items, whatever else in game and with other users if you want.
