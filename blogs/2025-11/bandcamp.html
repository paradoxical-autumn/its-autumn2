moving my music to bandcamp

TL;DR:

hey nerds, quick update

remember justAnotherParadox? yeah? okay, well. im pulling all that music from streaming (minus YOUTUBE) and moving it all to bandcamp

Why?

I've been thinking about this for a while now and its mainly boiled down to a few things. I wont bore you, so I'll keep it to a nice, consice list

  1. Raising prices
  2. Lacking numbers
  3. Monetary abuse

Raising prices

My music distributor (for those who don't know, uploading music to the internet requires a hellish corporation called a "distributor," since platforms don't want to create their own upload button. They can charge, what they want, whenever they want. There are multiple, but, I don't like any of 'em)

Wait, where was I again?

My music distributor (not naming names), had decided to raise the price of my subsription by £15/month, for an artist that barely makes money in the first place. Ouch.

Nothing much, but, the first nail in the coffin.

Anyway, yeah - no numbers are being pulled from streaming, so, might as well cut 'em because i need money as i am living in a capitalistic society

Now, there is one exclusion to this - YouTube (and YouTube music) - this was pulling about 30 per month? And that's long after release. So, I'm keeping my shit there - mainly as its also FREEEEEEE

Monetary abuse

hey quick q how much money do you think ive made from music distribution?

actually, the GROSS profits over these past 2 years?

£50? £25? £10? £5?

GROSS PROFIT: £2.36

i mean, i dont stream much, but, thats still somethi--

DISTRIBUTION COST (-£32.00 per year) * 2: -£64.00
INCOME:                                    £02.36
-------------------------------------------------
NET PROFIT:                               -£61.64

Well, that's not too good. It costs me more to leave my music online. So, I hope you now see my main reason for removing myself from distribution

(oh also to even be able to "cash out" you need to make at least £10 GROSS, which makes sense :P)

"But.. surely you could change distributors?!"

i could. and theyd probably be cheaper.

i wont though. for a few other reasons.
